Jovencourt Daiquiri

2 ounces Barbancourt White Rum
.25 ounce Del Maguey Vida Mezcal
.75 ounce Lime Juice
.75 ounce Simple Syrup

Shake all the ingredients with ice, then strain into a coupe. No garnish.
