Gilda Cocktail

2 ounces Siembra Azul Blanco Tequila
.5 ounce Pineapple Juice
.5 ounce Lime Juice
.5 ounce Cinnamon Bark Syrup
Wheel of Lime (Garnish)

Shake all the ingredients with ice, then strain into a coupe. Garnish with the lime wheel.
